⚠️ Safety Reminders
🔴Most dangerous gas is the one that doesn't burn. Never leave burner running unattended.
🔓Open kiln: higher radiant heat loss and splash risk — wear full face shield and leather apron. Keep bystanders back 3 m.
🌡️Shell must reach red-hot before pour — all moisture must be gone or it cracks violently.
🧪Dip test: cold iron rod in melt 2–3 sec. No bronze sticking = ready to pour.
🪨Add a piece of coke to bind free oxygen and prevent surface oxidation.
🔧Gas connectors use left-hand threads — prevents accidental link to O₂ (= explosion).
⚖️Maximum safe single-operator load: 15 kg. Never exceed alone.
